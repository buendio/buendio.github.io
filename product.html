why terrelique?

Ancient Tradition

Inspired by the ancient global tradition of taking shoes off inside, Terrelique was born to manifest the trust, love and ease from feeling actively included in a space.

OUR STORY

Showing Respect

Leaving shoes at the door when entering someone's home is a universal sign of respect and cleanliness. Terreliques stop dirt from tracking in and upgrade your look at the same time!

Reclaim Your Space

Keeping house slippers for guests lets them step into the special, curated experience of your space. Terreliques empower you to reclaim and elevate your unique sanctuary.

Inside Out

We love a house shoe, but Terreliques can take you anywhere, anytime. Choose your own adventure!

Massaging Footbed

Terreliques work hard to absorb shock on hard floors while also massaging the bottoms of your feet!

Terrelique was born to manifest the trust, love, and ease that accompany feeling not only welcome, but actively included in a space.
House slippers (for everyone and their guests) are an ancient practice in Turkey and numerous other countries such as Japan, Malaysia and Korea. When inviting guests over, hosts use this word to let them know they can truly breathe and make themselves at home.
We believe a shoe-free home empowers us to reclaim, curate, and share the experience of our sacred space.

We work exclusively with a family owned factory in Gaziantep, Turkey to support the country's tradition in sharing it with you.
Welcome to the shoe-free home movement.
